Featured Projects



OAKTON PARADISE

Located in a beautiful Oakton neighborhood, this project is a true hidden gem. The project breathed new life into the existing screened-in porch with all new finishes, new appliances, and a pavilion attachment with a wood burning fireplace. The pool was built nearly 10 feet out of ground surrounded by a deck supported pool deck to connect everything at one level. The finished result completely transformed the way this family uses their property and boosted their lifestyle.
